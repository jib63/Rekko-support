Support

A quick way to reach a human about Rekko.

Rekko is an indie app — there is no help-desk team and no chatbot, just one developer. The fastest way to get help is to send an email; replies usually go out within 48 hours, often the same day.

Contact

Email support-rekko@jibstudios.com for any of:

  • Bug reports (include your iOS / tvOS version + a short description of what happened).
  • Feature requests and ideas.
  • iCloud / sync questions.
  • Circle invitation issues.
  • Subscription, Founders pact, or refund questions.

For privacy or data requests

Anything related to your personal data — exports, deletion, RGPD / GDPR requests — goes to a different inbox so it doesn't get mixed in with general support traffic. Write to jib@jibstudios.com. See the privacy policy for the full list of rights and the response timeline (max 30 days, usually much less).

Before you write

A few quick fixes that resolve most of the questions we receive:

  • "My circle isn't syncing." Check that iCloud Drive is turned on for the same Apple ID on every device, and that the app is signed in to Sign in with Apple with that same identifier. The vast majority of "sync is broken" reports turn out to be one device on a different iCloud account.
  • "Where to watch doesn't open the right streamer." Rekko hands off to the Apple TV app, which resolves the streamer itself. If the wrong streamer opens, it's an Apple TV setting — open the Apple TV app → Settings → Connect Apps and re-link your accounts.
  • "I can't add a tenth show." Free accounts can track up to 10 series or movies. Upgrade to Premium in Settings to lift the cap.
